Blending evocative music, rich storytelling, and stunning visuals, this brand new opera promises to be a transformative experience!

This production is a labor of love and shares a significant moment in U.S. history that is not widely known. The Camp allows us to bring this story to life—from one sentence in our history books to a full story centered around a Japanese American family’s experience in America’s concentration camp during WWII.

Although the support we have received so far has been generous, we still need your support. Your contribution will help fund everything from set construction to rehearsals with world-class performers. Every donation—big or small—plays a vital role in making this production a reality.
